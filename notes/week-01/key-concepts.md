# Week 01 — Key Concepts

## PKI Explanation (3–5 Sentences)

Write a clear explanation of what PKI is in your own words.

PKI solves a trust problem on the internet. The role of the keys is to enable encryption and create digital signatures. The role of the certificate is to prove the server's identity by providing information that the browser can check to ensure it is valid, not revoked, not expired, and trusted. Trust is established through the process of validation, where the browser checks the server certificate and follows the chain up to a trusted root, ensuring each certificate is correcctly issued, valid, not revoked.
